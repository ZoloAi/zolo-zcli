Week 6.11.3.1: ‚úÖ COMPLETE - REMOVED String-Based Submissions
A+ ARCHITECTURAL PURITY
üìã Scope: Streamlined zDialog to pure declarative paradigm (dict-based only)
üîó Dependencies: dialog_submit.py (removed string handling)
üìä Impact: 100% aligned with zCLI's declarative philosophy (zUI, zSchema, zConfig all use YAML dicts)
‚è∞ Completed: v1.5.4 (immediate removal, zero users = zero pain)
üéØ RATIONALE:
  • Paradigm Violation: String-based submissions mix data and code, violating zCLI's pure declarative approach
  • Scalability: Complex submissions become ugly and hard to maintain as strings
  • Consistency: All other zCLI components (zUI, zSchema, zConfig) use structured YAML/dict declarations
  • Tooling: Dict-based enables IDE validation, autocomplete, and static analysis
  • YAML-Friendly: Dict-based submissions are natural in YAML, strings require escaping
üìä COMPARISON:
‚ùå String-Based (Legacy - To Be Deprecated):
